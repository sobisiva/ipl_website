History of CSK

Chennai Super Kings (CSK), established in 2008, is one of the most successful teams in IPL history. Known for their consistency, they have qualified for the playoffs in almost every season.

Matches Won

CSK has played 228 matches and won 133, maintaining an impressive win percentage.

Cups Won

CSK has won the IPL championship 5 times: 2010, 2011, 2018, 2021, and 2023.

Legendary Players

MS Dhoni, Suresh Raina, Dwayne Bravo, and Mike Hussey are among the legends who have contributed immensely to CSK's success.
